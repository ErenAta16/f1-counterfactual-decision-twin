## Decisions recorded

1. The project is named **ApexMind: F1 Counterfactual Decision Twin**.
2. v1 is an offline research prototype, not a live race-control or pit-wall system.
3. The first tactical decision space is limited to pit timing, tyre choice, stint extension, and track-position preservation.
4. Energy availability and active-aero effects are scenario variables in v1 because public telemetry does not reveal their true state.
5. Computer vision, CFD, reinforcement learning, and private-data integration are deferred until the core simulator passes its gates.
6. The explanation layer is constrained by optimiser output and retrieved sources; it cannot author strategy calculations.
